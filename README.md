# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a messy e-commerce dataset. The dataset contains customer information, orders, products, and order items with missing values, duplicates, and inconsistent data formats.

The objective of this project is to clean the data, transform it into a usable format, and generate meaningful business insights through visualizations and statistical analysis.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean messy datasets
- Handle missing values
- Remove duplicate records
- Convert incorrect data types
- Merge multiple datasets
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Generate business insights using visualizations

---

## 📂 Dataset

The project uses multiple datasets:

- Customers
- Orders
- Order Items
- Products

The datasets include information such as:

- Customer Details
- Order Details
- Product Information
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Payment Method
- Order Date
- Sales

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Data Cleaning Performed

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Converted date columns into proper datetime format
- Converted incorrect numeric columns
- Checked statistical summaries
- Merged multiple datasets into a single dataframe

## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis

The project includes analysis on:

- Total Sales
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Year-wise Sales
- Product-wise Sales
- Category-wise Sales
- City-wise Sales
- Quantity Sold
- Top 5 Customers by Revenue
- Top 5 Customers by Quantity
- Payment Method Distribution
- Correlation Heatmap

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- 💰 Total Revenue: **₹180,307,069**
- 📅 Highest Sales Year: **2024**
- 📈 Highest Monthly Sales: **January**
- 💻 Highest Selling Products:
  - Dell Inspiron
  - Laptop Bag
- 📱 Highest Selling Category: **Smartphone**
- 💼 Least Selling Category: **Laptop**
- 🏙️ Delhi recorded the highest quantity sold.
- 👤 Customer **C0218** generated the highest revenue and purchased the highest quantity.
- 💳 UPI contributed the highest share of sales.
- 📊 Quantity and Sales have a strong positive correlation (≈0.76).

   📋 ANALYSIS

💻 **Dell Inspiron** generated the **highest revenue** among all products, making it the company's top-performing product in terms of revenue contribution.

📱 **Smartphones** recorded the **highest sales quantity** among all product categories, indicating strong customer demand and market preference.

🏙️ **Delhi** achieved the **highest overall sales**, making it the company's strongest regional market and a key location for business growth.

🏆 From a product perspective, **Dell Inspiron** is the **highest revenue-generating product**, while **Smartphones** remain the most successful product category based on sales volume.

👥 **Customers C0218 and C0474** contributed the **highest revenue**, identifying them as the company's most valuable customers who should be targeted through loyalty and retention programs.

💳 **Credit Card payments** accounted for the **largest share of transactions**, indicating customers' preference for cashless payment methods.

📅 **January** recorded the **highest monthly sales**, suggesting strong seasonal demand and the need for increased inventory before peak periods.

📈 The analysis shows that a **small group of products and customers contributes a significant portion of total revenue**, highlighting opportunities for focused marketing and customer relationship management.

📦 Fast-selling products require **efficient inventory management** to avoid stock shortages and maintain customer satisfaction.



Business Recommendations
1) Focus on smartphones by maintaining sufficient inventory and launching new models.
2) Bundle best-selling products to increase the average order value.
3) Offer discounts and promotions for low-performing products to boost sales.
4) Increase marketing efforts in high-revenue cities like Delhi.
5) Introduce loyalty programs to retain high-value customers.
6) Promote digital payments through cashback, EMI, and exclusive offers.
7) Stock inventory in advance for peak sales periods, especially January.
8) Use sales forecasting to optimize inventory and reduce excess stock.
9) Develop real-time dashboards to monitor sales, customers, and product performance.
10) Use data-driven insights to improve marketing, inventory management, customer satisfaction, and overall business profitability.
